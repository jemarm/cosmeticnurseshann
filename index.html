ABOUT
SHANNEA

I'm a nurse with a love for nutrition, and I'm passionate about helping others achieve their health goals.

My journey towards becoming a nutritionist began after I had my two children. Like many new mothers, I struggled to lose the weight I had gained during pregnancy. Despite trying various diets and exercise programs, I found it difficult to maintain a healthy weight and lifestyle.

Through my own experiences and my education, I have learned the importance of a balanced and sustainable approach to nutrition. I am passionate about helping others develop healthy habits that can be maintained for life. Whether you're looking to lose weight, improve your energy levels, or simply lead a healthier lifestyle, I am here to support you every step of the way.

I look forward to working with you and helping you achieve your health goals!
